# Template Research Notes

The template gallery uses award-site research as high-level inspiration only. It does not copy winning site code, layouts, screenshots, or assets.

## Sources Reviewed

- Awwwards Sites of the Day and Winning Websites: interaction-heavy, visual-first, motion-led brand work.
- Awwwards Interaction Design gallery: meaningful engagement, immersive UX, motion and interaction quality.
- CSS Design Awards and CSS Winner: UI, UX, innovation scores, cinematic luxury and polished contemporary sites.
- Webby 2026 Websites and Mobile Sites winners: category breadth, Best Home Page, art/design, data visualization, and cause storytelling.

## Patterns Converted Into Original Templates

- Museum/editorial art direction: restrained navigation, archival captions, dramatic cropping.
- Cinematic luxury: dark atmospheric framing, scarcity, concierge action.
- Data storytelling: insight cards, map-like signals, narrative evidence.
- Kinetic agency: bold type, motion-inspired strips, capability chips.
- Immersive product launch: product orbit, feature hotspots, drop/preorder flow.
- Human cause campaign: emotional hierarchy, timeline evidence, action prompts.
